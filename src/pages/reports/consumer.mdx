### Product Bundling

Offer bundled packages of home appliances at discounted prices, allowing consumers to purchase a complete set of appliances for their homes. This strategy can include bundles for kitchen appliances, laundry appliances, or even whole-home packages.

### Subscription-based Services

Explore the possibility of offering subscription-based services for appliance maintenance, repairs, or even appliance rentals. This can provide a recurring revenue stream and foster long-term customer relationships.


### Personalization and Customization

Implement a configurator that allows consumers to personalize and customize their appliances based on their preferences, such as color, finishes, or additional features. This can enhance the perceived value and create a unique buying experience.
